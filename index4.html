PakShaheens.com is a focused, student-first platform built to help aspiring cadets and defence professionals prepare effectively for selection into Pakistan’s Army, Navy, and Air Force. Combining practical study material, experience-backed mentorship, and realistic training plans, PakShaheens streamlines the path from preparation to passing.

About PakShaheens

PakShaheens began with a simple mission: make reliable defence preparation accessible to every motivated youth in Pakistan and beyond. Today it offers a wide range of resources — from ISSB mock exercises and intelligence test papers to physical training routines and interview coaching — all crafted by retired officers, trainers, and subject experts.

Core Services

  • ISSB & Psych Prep: Mock tests, GTO simulations, and personality-building exercises.
  • Academic Tests: Topic-synced practice for English, Math, Islamiat, and General Knowledge.
  • Physical Training Plans: Progressive running, strength circuits, and nutrition basics for selection fitness.
  • Interview & CV Coaching: Practical tips, common questions, and one-on-one mentorship options.

How We Help Aspirants

Preparation is more than content — it’s practice, feedback, and mindset. PakShaheens provides curated daily routines, timed mock tests to build speed, and mentor reviews that highlight areas to improve. The platform emphasizes consistent small wins: improved run times, higher mock scores, and greater confidence in interviews.

“A purpose-built preparation plan combined with real mentorship is what converts ambition into a commission.”
